About me

Hello! I'm Kim Burda, a cybersecurity professional currently based in Colorado. I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Applied Science in Cybersecurity from Pikes Peak State College, where I maintained a 4.0 GPA throughout both my associate's and bachelor's degrees.

My experience spans both offensive and defensive security — from SOC operations and threat hunting to penetration testing and threat intelligence. I hold the HTB Certified Web Exploitation Specialist (CWES) and multiple certifications from Cisco, CompTIA, and ISC2, with hands-on experience using tools like Burp Suite, Splunk, Wireshark, Nmap, and Metasploit.

With roots in the Czech Republic and years spent between Europe and the United States, I have had the opportunity to travel and immerse myself in multiple cultures and languages — including Czech, Slovak, Polish, and English, with Norwegian currently in progress.

Feel free to explore my website to learn more!
