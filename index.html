About me

Hello! I'm Kim Burda, a 20-year-old Computer Science and Cybersecurity major currently attending the University of Colorado Colorado Springs, where I am pursuing my Bachelor's degree. I currently hold multiple certifications from Cisco, CompTIA, and ISC2, and I plan on pursuing additional certifications in the upcoming years.

As for Computer Science, I am proficient in multiple languages, including Python, C, and Java. Security-wise, I am proficient with tools such as Nmap, Wireshark, and Metasploit, as well as any other tools Kali Linux holds. Other than Kali, I have experience working with other major Linux distributions, Windows, Mac OS, and Cisco IOS.

Some notable projects I have undertaken include creating a Medical Record System in Java and programming a Raspberry Pi as an Evil Twin Attack device. Currently, I am working on other projects, including this website which you are on right now, as well as an Outfit Planner Website/App using Django and Babylon.js.

While I am currently based in Colorado, Colorado Springs, I have had the opportunity to travel to multiple countries which has allowed me to become proficient in Czech, Slovak, Polish, English, and more. I am also in the process of learning Norwegian.
